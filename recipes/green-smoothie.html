Green Smoothie

Description

This healthy green smoothie is guaranteed to give you a fresh start to your day - easy and delightful!

Ingredients

  • 20g baby spinach leaves
  • 1 cup Coles Frozen Pineapple Pieces
  • 1 kiwifruit, peeled, chopped
  • 1/2 cup (125ml) almond or coconut milk or dairy-free milk
  • 1/2 cup chopped banana

Steps

  1. Place spinach, pineapple, kiwifruit, banana and milk in a clean blender and blend until smooth. Pour into a serving glass.
  2. Serve the smoothie immediately.
